"I believe good design solves business problems, not just visual problems."

Hi, I'm Stephanie Dahunsi, a UI/UX Designer and Frontend Web Developer passionate about creating digital experiences that are simple, intuitive and meaningful. I enjoy turning ideas into products that not only look good but also solve real problems for the people using them.

My journey started with a curiosity about how technology works, but I quickly found myself drawn to the people behind the screens. That curiosity led me into UI/UX design, and eventually into frontend development, where I can bring my designs to life with clean, responsive code.

Today, I help businesses and startups transform ideas into user-friendly websites and digital products, combining thoughtful design with functional development to create experiences people genuinely enjoy.
